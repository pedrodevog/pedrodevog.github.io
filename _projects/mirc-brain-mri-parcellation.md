## Overview

During a six-week research internship (summer 2024) at the Medical Imaging Research Center (MIRC), KU Leuven, I worked on automated parcellation of lesioned brain images.

## Context

- **Institution:** Medical Imaging Research Center (MIRC), KU Leuven
- **Supervisors:** Prof. Frederik Maes and Dr. Ahmed Radwan
- **Domain:** Clinical MRI analysis and segmentation

## Contribution

I contributed to the implementation and evaluation of segmentation pipelines for magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) data, with a focus on robust methodology development and testing on clinical imaging datasets.

## Outcome

The work supported ongoing research outputs, including manuscript preparation with collaborators.

## Focus Areas

- Medical image segmentation
- MRI pipeline implementation and evaluation
- Reproducible methodology for clinical imaging data
